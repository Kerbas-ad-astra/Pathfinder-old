# Prospector  
The Rockhound has a Prospector that has the following recipe:  
**Inputs**  
_Rock:_ 5.0 units/sec  
_ElectricCharge:_ 15.0 units/sec  
**Output**  
_RareMetals:_ 0.015 unit/sec  
_ExoticMinerals:_ 0.015 unit/sec
